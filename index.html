About Us

Samaj Kalyan Organization is a Section 8 Company-based NGO working in India for the upliftment of underprivileged communities. Our focus areas include education, healthcare, women empowerment, and livelihood generation. We are committed to building a just and inclusive society through sustainable interventions and impactful programs.

Our Key Programs

  • Child Education Initiative – School readiness and bridge education centers for out-of-school children.
  • Mobile Health Clinics – Delivering basic healthcare and screenings to remote villages.
  • Livelihood for Women – Vocational training and self-employment support for rural women.
  • Disaster Relief – Emergency response and rehabilitation during floods, pandemics, and natural calamities.
